About the Project

Project Zechus Avos is a project to republish the groundbreaking sefer Mekor Habracha, written by the Skolya Rebbe's grandfather, R' Dovid Yitzchak Isaac. The sefer is a commentary on the first 3 words of the torah, and he darshens 42 mitzvos from those 3 words. It is a must-have for anyone interested in the deeper meaning of the torah. With your help, we can make this project a reality. Sponsor a chapter today!
