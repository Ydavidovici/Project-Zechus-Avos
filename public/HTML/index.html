    • Welcome to Project Zechus Avos

    Project Zechus Avos is dedicated to the republishing of seminal works of Chassidic thought and Kabbalah, offering a unique opportunity for individuals to engage with and sponsor these profound teachings. Our primary goal is to make these deep insights accessible and allow for personal involvement through sponsorship.

    "Mekor Habracha" is a groundbreaking sefer written on only the first three words of the Torah, yet it uncovers 42 mitzvot, providing an extensive depth of commentary on each one. "Siach Eliezer" by the Yampoli Rebbe is an equally deep work exploring themes within Sefer Bereshis, Shemos, Vayikra Tzav, and Shemini.
