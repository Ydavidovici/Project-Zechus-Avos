Welcome to Project Zechus Avos

Project Zechus Avos, spearheaded by the Ostroha-Skolya Chassidus based in Airmont, New York, is dedicated to the republishing of seminal works of Chassidic thought and Kabbalah. This initiative, under the guidance of the Skolye Rebbe, seeks to make profound teachings accessible for personal and communal growth.

Learn more about our community and activities at ostraha.org.

About Mekor Habracha

"Mekor Habracha" offers a profound exploration of the first three words of the Torah, from which the Skolye Rebbe derives forty-two Mitzvos. This Sefer provides unique insights into how foundational concepts of Emunah and Mitzvos are intricately woven into the fabric of the Torah's opening Pasuk. Its commentary deepens understanding and appreciation of the Torah's spiritual depth, making it essential reading for those seeking a deeper connection with Chassidus.

About Siach Eliezer

"Siach Eliezer" is an exceptional work of Chassidus and Kabbalah that delves into Sefer Bereishis, Sefer Shemos, and select Parshas of Vayikra (Tzav and Shemini). Authored by Rebbe Eliezer Chaim of Yampoli, this Sefer explores deep Kabbalistic teachings embedded within the Torah’s narratives and laws. Its discussions illuminate the hidden dimensions of Judaism, providing readers with transformative insights into their Ruchni and Gashmi lives.

Haskamos

"Mekor Habracha" has strong Haskamon from many Gedolim:

  • Chaim Shmulevitz: "A very clear explanation of many hidden concepts."
  • Yaakov Yisroel Kanievsky: "With holy intellect and sharpness, he is able to explain deep foundations."
